Immediate Effects (Day 0)

The impact creates immediate catastrophic effects:

  • Fireball formation exceeding 5,000°C
  • Supersonic shockwave propagation
  • Ground zero vaporization
  • Seismic waves triggering earthquakes
  • Thermal radiation igniting fires

Secondary Effects (Hours to Days)

Following the initial impact:

  • Global atmospheric shockwave
  • Ejected material raining down
  • Regional firestorms
  • Infrastructure collapse
  • Communication disruption

Long-term Consequences (Weeks to Months)

Lasting environmental effects:

  • Atmospheric dust blocking sunlight
  • Temperature drop affecting agriculture
  • Potential impact winter
  • Ecosystem disruption
  • Global economic impact
